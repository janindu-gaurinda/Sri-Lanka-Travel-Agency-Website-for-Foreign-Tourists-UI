Hiking to Ella Rock

Title: Hiking to Ella Rock

Excerpt: A must-do hike offering stunning views of tea estates and mountain valleys.

Content:Hiking to Ella Rock is a breathtaking adventure that rewards you with panoramic views of lush tea plantations, rolling hills, and distant waterfalls.
The trail starts near Ella railway station and winds through peaceful countryside, local vegetable gardens, and hidden viewpoints.
While the climb is moderately challenging, the sunrise at the summit is well worth the effort.

This is a perfect experience for nature lovers and photographers seeking a unique Sri Lankan adventure beyond the usual tourist spots.
